VMStock Privacy Policy

Your Privacy Matters: VMStock is designed with privacy-first principles. Most of your data stays on your device, and we only collect what's necessary to provide our services.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Data You Provide

When you use VMStock, you may provide:

  • Product Information: Product names, prices, stock quantities, categories
  • Player Data: Player names, balance information, transaction history
  • Sales Records: Assignment details, payment status, transaction amounts
  • Organization Settings: Business name, currency preferences, operational settings

1.2 Automatically Collected Data

Data Type Purpose Storage Location
Device Information App optimization and sync identification Local device only
Sync Metadata Multi-device synchronization Firebase Cloud Firestore
Usage Analytics App performance improvement Anonymous, local processing
Error Logs Bug fixes and stability Local device only

2. How We Use Your Information

2.1 Primary Purposes

  • Core Functionality: Provide point-of-sale operations, inventory management, and sales tracking
  • Data Synchronization: Enable multi-device access to your business data
  • Payment Processing: Facilitate secure payment transactions through Stripe
  • Offline Operations: Ensure app functionality without internet connectivity

2.2 Data Processing Principles

Offline-First Architecture: VMStock processes most data locally on your device. Cloud synchronization occurs in the background to enable multi-device access while maintaining privacy.

3. Data Storage and Security

3.1 Local Storage

  • Primary Storage: All business data is stored locally on your device using secure SQLite databases
  • Encryption: Local data is encrypted using industry-standard AES-256 encryption
  • Access Control: Data is protected by device-level security (passcode, biometrics)

3.2 Cloud Storage (Firebase)

  • Purpose: Multi-device synchronization and backup
  • Security: Data transmitted via HTTPS with end-to-end encryption
  • Access: Firebase security rules restrict access to your data only
  • Location: Data stored in Google Cloud Platform data centers

3.3 Payment Data Security

PCI DSS Compliance: Payment processing is handled exclusively by Stripe, a PCI DSS Level 1 certified provider. VMStock never stores credit card information.
  • Payment Processing: All payment data handled by Stripe's secure infrastructure
  • Transaction Records: Only transaction IDs and status stored in VMStock
  • No Card Storage: Credit card numbers never stored on device or in cloud

4. Data Sharing and Third Parties

4.1 Service Providers

  • Firebase (Google): Cloud storage and synchronization
    • Purpose: Multi-device data sync
    • Data: Business records, sync metadata
    • Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy
  • Stripe: Payment processing
    • Purpose: Secure payment transactions
    • Data: Payment information during transaction only
    • Privacy Policy: https://stripe.com/privacy

4.2 No Data Sales

We Never Sell Your Data: VMStock does not sell, rent, or share your personal or business data with third parties for marketing purposes.

5. Your Privacy Rights

5.1 Data Control

  • Full Control: You maintain complete control over your business data
  • Local Access: Access all data directly on your device, even offline
  • Export Capability: Export your data in standard formats (CSV, JSON)
  • Deletion Rights: Delete individual records or all data at any time

5.2 Account Management

  • Data Portability: Request a copy of all your data
  • Account Deletion: Request complete account and data deletion
  • Sync Control: Enable/disable cloud synchronization at any time
  • Privacy Settings: Customize data sharing preferences

6. Data Retention

6.1 Retention Periods

  • Business Data: Retained until you choose to delete it
  • Transaction Records: Retained for 7 years for tax/accounting purposes
  • Sync Metadata: Retained for 30 days after last device sync
  • Error Logs: Automatically deleted after 30 days

6.2 Deletion Process

Complete Deletion: When you delete data in VMStock, it's permanently removed from both your device and cloud storage within 30 days.

7. International Data Transfers

If you use VMStock outside your home country:

  • Cross-Border Sync: Data may be transmitted between countries for multi-device access
  • Cloud Storage: Firebase data centers located globally, with appropriate safeguards
  • Legal Compliance: All transfers comply with GDPR, CCPA, and local privacy laws

8. Children's Privacy

VMStock is designed for business use and is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

9. Changes to This Policy

  • Updates: We may update this policy to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements
  • Notification: Material changes will be communicated through the app or email
  • Continued Use: Continued use of VMStock after changes constitutes acceptance

10. Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR)

For users in the European Union, our legal basis for processing your data:

  • Contractual Necessity: To provide VMStock services you've requested
  • Legitimate Interests: To improve app performance and security
  • Consent: For optional features like analytics (where required by law)
